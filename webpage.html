Visiting the Jewel at Changi Airport,
Singapore

The Jewel is a huge shopping mall and entertainment complex
located landside at Singapore's Changi airport. There's much more
to it than that though as it is home to the world's tallest indoor
waterfall that is known as the Rain Vortex. This stunning waterfall
is surrounded by lush tropical gardens with terraced seating.
